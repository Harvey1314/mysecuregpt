What are the risks of Secure
ChatGPT? And how do we address
them?

Take a journey into the security infrastructure of MySecureGPT, featuring local hosting and internet isolation to fortify against potential data breaches, setting new standards for secure conversational AI.

Like any powerful technology, Secure ChatGPT introduces risks if deployed without controls. The most common concerns include unauthorized access to sensitive information, accidental exposure of internal data through prompts, over-reliance on AI outputs, and compliance risks when data is processed in unsecured environments.

MySecureGPT addresses these risks through a layered security approach. We support secure deployment patterns such as local/private hosting, network isolation, and strict access controls, ensuring your organization’s data stays within your governed environment.

In addition, administrative policies and guardrails help reduce data leakage. Role-based access, audit trails, and retention controls ensure that usage is accountable and aligned with your security requirements.

Finally, we encourage a “human-in-the-loop” workflow for critical use cases. This ensures AI-assisted work remains accurate, safe, and compliant—while still delivering speed and productivity benefits.
